Interactive USGS Funding Map and Bar Charts

By Nick Kolarik

Description

Interactive map and charts of total funding for USGS Water Science Centers by state during the year 2015. The data is provided by the United States Geological Survey (USGS). The choropleth map shows total funding by state in the midwest region. The bar charts show the funding by state in the midwest region broken down into component parts of appropriated funds, other mission area funds, and reimbursable funds.
